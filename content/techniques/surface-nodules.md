**What are the dark bumps on these pots?** Small hard nodules that sit proud of the surface instead of blending in — from scattered single grains ([[1735]]) to clustered patches ([[1730]], [[1733]]) to a full pebbled crust ([[1742]], [[1745]]). The store's titles for three of these pieces name the material as Lake Erie shale; this site quotes that without confirming it — the photos show texture, not chemistry.

Eleven pieces in the [[range-1727-1769|black stoneware run]] carry it.
