**There's writing on these cups — what does it say?** Two of the [[range-2085-2092|abstract cups]] carry handwritten words in dark glaze over their [[brushed-glaze|brushed decoration]]. On [[2089]], fragments like "ska…" and "Skati…" repeat around the wall; on [[2092]], both sides carry lines — "I", "keep", "it's nothin…", "I was…".

That's as far as the photos go. These pages quote the words exactly as far as they're legible and stop there — what the lines mean, and what the hidden parts say, stays with the maker and with whoever ends up holding the cup.
