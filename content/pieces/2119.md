Sibling to [[2118]]'s stony idiom, but [[handbuilding|handbuilt]] through and through: a rounded belly narrowing to a small uneven foot that sits with daylight under one edge. The milky grey-white coating thins to bare tan at the wobbling rim; the interior well is peppered with dark speckles; two pit-holes and a tiny star-shaped crack mark the lower flank, with crawl-crack veining at the shoulder — all texture, all recorded plainly. The base is a hand-flattened bare disc with [[numbered-bases|"2119" handwritten]] in dark ink.

**Does it sit flat?** Almost — the foot rides up at one edge, part of the same hand-built lean the whole cup carries. Piece 2119 of 10,000, listed at $21.19.
