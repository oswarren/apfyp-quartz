The most graphic of the painted cups. A white ground carries a row of bold black [[brushed-glaze|brush dashes]] — blocky marks stacked almost like a barcode — laid over blue-grey washes and dark speckle, some of the black gone glossy where it thickened. The strokes are unmistakably brushed, discrete and deliberate. The base carries [[numbered-bases|"2170" handwritten]] in black.

**Is that writing?** It reads as a rhythm of black dashes, not letters — bold brushwork recorded exactly as strokes and never decoded, the third of the hand-painted trio with [[2159]] and [[2167]]. Piece 2170 of 10,000, listed at $21.70.
