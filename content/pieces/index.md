Every piece in this series has a number, and every number has a price: **the piece number times one cent**. Piece #1 was a penny. Piece #10,000 will be $100.00. This section is the ledger — one page per piece, from the founding pieces of March 2023 to the newest work.

## What's listed here (and what isn't)

**Only photographed pieces get a page.** Most of the 10,000 numbers aren't here yet — some pieces exist but haven't been photographed for their listings, and thousands of numbers are still unmade, waiting as [reservations on the store](https://apennyforyourpottery.com). A number that's missing here isn't gone; it just hasn't had its picture taken.

Pages here never claim a piece is in stock or sold — every page links to its live Shopify listing, which is the only source of truth for availability, shipping, and checkout.

## Reserve a number

Numbers that haven't been made yet can be reserved on the store — buying a number is buying the right to that piece when the series reaches it. That happens [on Shopify](https://apennyforyourpottery.com), not here.
