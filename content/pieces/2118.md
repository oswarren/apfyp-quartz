The range changes register: after seventeen painted cups comes a squat barrel that photographs like a riverbank stone. A thin milky sheen pools glossy at the rim and leaves matte bare sandy patches mid-flank; fine red-brown crazing veins spider one face; an iron-orange scorch line rings the foot; dark speckles pepper rim and interior. The walls dent and bulge, but the base disc shows clean concentric trimming rings, with [[numbered-bases|"2118" handwritten]] across them.

**Why does it look wet?** The sheen — a translucent coat that goes glassy where it thickens at the rim, exactly like a stone pulled from the water. Piece 2118 of 10,000, listed at $21.18.
