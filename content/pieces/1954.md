A [[handbuilding|handbuilt]] cup like a river stone — grey-beige, [[sand-speckled|peppered with dark grit]] through the whole surface — with a rough little [[pulled-handle|pulled loop]] attached at the side and a warm rust band crossing the belly. The pinched rim carries glossy drips; the interior goes dark and wet-looking. After 1,953 pieces without one, a handle.

**Why does a handle matter here?** Because the catalog had never shown one — this cup starts a family that runs through [[range-1951-2000|the rest of the run]]. Piece 1954 of 10,000, listed at $19.54.
