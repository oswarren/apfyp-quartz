The boldest black in the splash family rides this plump-bellied [[handbuilding|handbuilt]] cup: one great ink-blot mass grips the rim and spills into the interior as a black cloud, another spreads mid-flank with satellite blobs, while the other face answers quietly with a navy diagonal streak, sky-blue wash, and a navy pool ringing the foot. Frost-white overspray hazes it all. The base shows [[numbered-bases|"2124" handwritten]] boldly in black, with blue specks at the disc's edge.

**How far in does the black go?** It hangs over the lip like a wave caught breaking — a hand-width of interior wall under black before the white takes over. Piece 2124 of 10,000, listed at $21.24.
