At a glance it wants to be an amphora — two rough [[pulled-handle|pulled ear loops]] high on the shoulders — but the mouth is a torn hole straight in the crown with no neck under it, so it files with the two-handled boulders like [[2032]]. The near-black charcoal body is the dark stage for white [[lichen-crust|mud-crack crust]]: a big rosette panel flakes on the belly with more patches at the shoulder, the pale plates lifting lichen-like off the dark ground — the inverse of [[2039]]'s white-on-warm-brown. Pink-tan sandy grit dusts the crown, grey-green speckled pebble inclusions sit in the flank, and faint pink tinges warm the crust. One loop carries a crack across its outer arc, left as fired and recorded here as-is.
