The only cup of the run that stands on a stem: a tall pedestal foot lifts the bowl like a small goblet. Around the body runs a matte sky-blue horizon band, broken by white cloud gaps where the blue never landed, and from it a single white drip falls and stops mid-wall, frozen halfway down. One flank takes a charcoal-violet crust patch with a torn edge. Under the carved foot: [[numbered-bases|"2143" handwritten]] in black.

**Why the tall foot?** No other cup from these dates has one — it turns a tumbler's profile into a goblet's, and the listing offers no explanation beyond the photos. Piece 2143 of 10,000, listed at $21.43.
