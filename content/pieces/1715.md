An upright, roughly cylindrical vessel that leans and swells as it rises, its walls carried up in irregular [[coil-ridges|horizontal ridges]] to a torn, uneven open mouth. The dark red-brown surface has gone to a [[beaded-crust|blistered, bead-covered crust]], and where the light rakes across the crown it lifts to a bright [[metallic-sheen|silvery sheen]] — salt-fired, the shine the firing left. Lower down, pale and golden [[embedded-particles|flecks]] surface through the crust. The leaning walls and torn rim read as [[handbuilding|handbuilt]]. It was photographed on a wooden sill against a workshop wall.
