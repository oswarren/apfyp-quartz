It is [[handbuilding|handbuilt]] beneath all that weather, and the crust's chemistry stays its own.

**Is the crust stable?** The photos can't say and this page won't — they show plates of pale crust with lifted edges, and stop there. Piece 1872 of 10,000, listed at $18.72.
