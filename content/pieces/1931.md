Thin dark lines wrap this cream cup like string wound loose — some run near-horizontal, some cross at diagonals, all with dry skips where they thin. It inverts the batch-9 look of [[white-threads|white threads on dark]]: here the threads are dark and the body pale. The lines' method isn't provable from the photo — trailed, inlaid, or drawn — so they carry no decoration tag; the pale grey [[glazed-rim-band|rim band]] above them does.
