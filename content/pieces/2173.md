The May-5 cups end on their busiest, best-documented piece — eight photos for one cup. The body is covered in a glossy speckled turquoise-blue glaze, crystalline and mottled, shot through with dark burst-spots that give it a starry look, with black gestural blobs and a few white gaps breaking the field. It is all glaze surface, reactive and pooling, rather than a drawn design. The base carries [[numbered-bases|"2173" handwritten]] in black.

**Why eight photos?** Because there's a lot to circle — a whole cup of speckled reactive blue, dark-spotted like a night sky, the most involved surface in the painted run. Piece 2173 of 10,000, listed at $21.73.
