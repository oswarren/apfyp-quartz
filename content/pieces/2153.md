The last of the April 30 trio trades gloss for a dry, chalky finish: a matte warm-white barrel cup, its exterior powdery rather than shiny, scattered with small iron tan-brown spots. On one side a dark sooty band crusts the rim, as if that edge stood closer to the heat. Tip it toward the light and the inside answers back — a glossy white glazed lining, the wet counterpoint to the dry outer wall. None of the three photos shows the base, so its number goes unrecorded here.

**Dry outside, wet inside?** That's the contrast the cup is built on — a matte iron-flecked shell with a scorched rim, wrapped around a smooth glassy white interior. Piece 2153 of 10,000, listed at $21.53.
