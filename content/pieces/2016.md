One ear, not two — which leaves this sandy pink-tan jar outside both the amphora and bottle columns of the ledger, a one-eared cousin with a single small pierced [[pulled-handle|loop]] at its shoulder. What it lacks in symmetry it spends on surface: the whole shoulder is webbed in [[lichen-crust|crazed pale crust]], flat mud-crack plates over the warm [[handbuilding|handbuilt]] body, purple-dark shadows pooling in the crack lines while pale drips run down the flank below.

**Why doesn't piece 2016 get a form name?** The registry's jars come two-handled or none — one ear fits neither box, so the shape stays prose and the crust does the identifying. Piece 2016 of 10,000, listed at $20.16.
