Piece 1942 is a cream-bodied cup with a defined, rounded lip, thrown on the wheel. A pale grey [[glazed-rim-band|band of glaze]] gathers at the rim and slides down the outside in long, narrow drips.

Low on the body, two dark [[brushed-glaze|brown brush-strokes]] cut across the pale clay, and a dimpled, dappled patch textures one side. Inside, small [[glaze-flecks|green and reddish flecks]] dot the pale glaze.

**Where does the colour collect on this cup?** Almost all of it rides the rim — a grey glaze that drips down the side — while the cream body below stays bare but for two brown strokes. Piece 1942 of 10,000, listed at $19.42.
