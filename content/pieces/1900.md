Piece 1900 stands tall and open-mouthed, its near-black charcoal walls pinched and uneven and its rim torn into a soft, ragged edge — a [[handbuilding|hand-formed]] vessel with no sign of the wheel. Two glassy [[blue-flecks|spots of blue]] sit toward the middle of one face, bright against the darkness, with a paler blue-grey crust drifting down from the rim. Chalky white deposits and a single mint-pale splash break the charcoal in a few other places.
