The strangest surface in the run's pale family: large brown shapes ghost across this cream cup like pressed leaves or flowers — feathered outlines, translucent centers, the look of something organic that touched the clay and left. A pearl-grey band droops off the rim, its edge running down over one shape.

**Is it a real leaf print?** It photographs exactly like one — but pressed plant, transferred print, or brushwork are all possible, so the page names the resemblance and stops there. The registry holds a leaf-print term on watch for a second case. Piece 1935 of 10,000, listed at $19.35.
