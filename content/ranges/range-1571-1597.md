Sixteen photographed pieces sit between numbers 1571 and 1597 — a stretch where the series moves through three clay looks, sometimes inside a single piece. Prices run \$15.71 to \$15.97 (each piece's number times one cent). The numbers missing between them simply have no photographed listing yet.

Three families show up in the photos:

- **Terracotta-red cups with a glazed rim** — warm red-brown [[bare-clay|bare clay]] bodies under a gray [[glazed-rim-band|rim band]]: [[1571]], [[1574]], [[1576]], [[1577]], [[1578]]
- **Marbled clay** — two clays folded visibly together: [[1579]], [[1580]] (an open bowl), [[1583]], [[1592]]
- **White-buff vessels** — pale pinched forms, mostly unglazed: [[1587]], [[1588]], [[1589]], [[1591]], [[1592]], [[1593]], [[1594]]

And one outlier: [[1597]], a coarse sandy pot with black patches.
