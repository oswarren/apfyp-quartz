Forty-six pieces from $22.01 to $22.49, with reserve gaps at 2224, 2234, and 2235. The range opens where the [[range-2151-2200|previous one]] left off and stays with the bowl: a May-12 group of thin-walled organic footed bowls, decorated with brushed slip and glaze — a black hash on marbled cream ([[2201]]), a black diamond ([[2203]]), and soft-to-bold tan and grey streaks ([[2206]], [[2207]], [[2208]], [[2209]]) — alongside earthier reactive surfaces, a rust crust ([[2202]]) and an iron-patch bowl ([[2205]]). The May-14 bowls that follow are rougher and more matte, and they find a signature in bold black [[brushed-glaze|brushwork]]: a grid ([[2212]]), diagonal sweeps ([[2213]], [[2217]], [[2218]], [[2219]]), and an angular little line-figure that recurs across three bowls ([[2214]], [[2215]], [[2228]]). Between them run quieter and stranger surfaces — an iron speckle spatter ([[2211]]), black feathery bursts ([[2216]]), reactive blue-grey mottles ([[2221]], [[2225]], [[2231]]), three bare raw-clay bowls ([[2226]], [[2229]], [[2232]]), a [[leaf-print|leaf impression]] revealed under a crawling dark glaze ([[2220]]), and a dramatic white [[lichen-crust|lichen crust]] ([[2227]]).

Then, at June, the range breaks hard. Pieces [[2236]]–[[2240]] and [[2243]] are coarse, sandy near-black bowls in black sculpture clay — bare [[sand-grit|sand-grit]] bodies with glossy dark glazed pools inside, one scored with an incised cross-hatch — with a pale iron-speckled pair ([[2241]], [[2242]]) set among them and a seven-photo marbled blue-white tea bowl ([[2244]]) under a crazed, peeling glaze. The range closes on a new surface: five encrusted "rock" cups wearing a thick pale [[beaded-crust|beaded crust]] — [[2245]] from June 8, then [[2246]], [[2247]], [[2248]], and [[2249]] from June 16 — a term added with this batch. The photos span four dates — May 12, May 14, June 8, and June 16, 2026 — and every listing photo in the range was reviewed up close on 2026-07-13. Piece [[2249]] links forward into [[2250]], where the human-reviewed pilot begins.
