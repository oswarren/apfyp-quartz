Forty-nine pieces from $21.01 to $21.50, with a single reserve gap at 2129. The range opens where the [[range-2051-2100|previous one]] left off — with brushwork: the [[brushed-glaze|dry-brushed]] navy pair [[2101]] and [[2102]], every bristle stroke visible. Right behind them the sandy family walks across the range boundary: [[2103]] pours the same milk-white liner into the same [[sand-grit|sand-rough]] body as the twins 2096 and 2097, [[2106]] wears a sand collar, and [[2108]] presses vein-intact [[leaf-print|leaf impressions]] all over its sandy barrel — the family that put `surface/sand-grit` in the registry.

The heart of the April 21 run is a long cream cup family painted in navy weather — ink blots ([[2104]], [[2109]], [[2112]]), black clouds and masses ([[2105]], [[2107]], [[2111]], [[2114]], [[2124]]), one bold stroke ([[2113]]), an archipelago ([[2116]]), stripes and a heart-pressed base ([[2117]]). Then the palette dries out into the stony bare run [[2118]]–[[2127]]: cups that photograph like riverbank stones — a glaze drip on bare clay ([[2120]]), a ribbed tumbler held in a hand ([[2122]]), a smoke-dark cup ([[2125]]), and the bone-bare, toast-rimmed [[2127]]. [[2128]] closes the pre-gap stretch with navy bands; 2129 is reserve, and the chain resumes at the slate-dark [[2130]].

The thirties turn blue — navy sweeps and turquoise bands ([[2131]]), the teal swath ([[2132]]) and its photo-sharing twin [[2133]], the all-over [[2134]], and [[2135]], whose [[blue-crust|blue crust]] rides beside a black lava tongue. The April 26 septet goes dark: the pitted black field ([[2137]]), the almost entirely black-dipped [[2138]], a rust drip ([[2139]]), a raised beaded panel ([[2140]]) — and then the range's headline, [[2141]], the writing cup, its [[incised-word|incised fragments]] putting `mark/incised-word` in the registry at last. A goblet-footed [[2143]] closes that date. The two May cups are the weathered [[2144]]/[[2145]] driftwood pair, and the April 30 closers wind the range down in white and pale blue: the plain barrel [[2146]], the sandy band [[2147]], a wobbling blue horizon ([[2148]]), one blue cloud ([[2149]]), and [[2150]]'s single comet stroke at $21.50.
