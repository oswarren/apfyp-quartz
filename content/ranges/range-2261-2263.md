Where [[range-2250-2260|the documented pilot range]] ends at 2260, the white family keeps going: three more [[handbuilding|handbuilt]] cups in torn, crusted cream-white clay, direct kin of the pilot cups one number over. Two of them ([[2262]], [[2263]]) were photographed in the same June 22, 2026 session as the pilot's second half — [[production-june-2026|the photography notes]] cover that day.

Prices run \$22.61 to \$22.63, and each cup has its own signature: [[2261]] wears patches of dark [[sand-speckled|grit speckle]] over gouged walls and stands on a spiral-coiled base; [[2262]] is the heaviest-crusted, with [[embedded-particles|pale chips embedded]] through its grit; [[2263]] trades speckle for a single large toasted umber blotch.
