This is the full range covered by this catalog pilot — 11 consecutive pieces from A Penny For Your Pottery's numbered ceramic series. Because each piece's price is exactly one cent times its piece number, this range is also the price neighborhood for these pieces: piece 2250 is \$22.50, piece 2260 is \$22.60, with no exceptions.

All 11 pieces share the same general treatment: small [[handbuilding|handbuilt]] cups with white/cream [[crackle-glaze|crackle glaze]] and dark [[speckled-surfaces|speckling]], each individually numbered on its base. The [[numbered-bases|marking style]] changes partway through this range, between pieces 2253 and 2254 — see [[production-june-2026]] for the production-date grouping.

## Before these were made

An earlier full-catalog export in this project's archive shows pieces in this range once listed differently — before they existed, as reservations:

> "This piece is not yet made. This is the right to reserve this piece. I found that as I progressed through these iterations on Instagram, people would ask about reserving without seeing them, as the surprise in itself seemed worth it. So, that's what this is. Please note: these may not be ready any time soon. I will send them sometime. Hopefully it coincides with a time when you forget about them."

That snapshot predates the real photos shown on this site — by the time these 11 pieces were photographed (June 16 and June 22, 2026), they had already moved from reservation to finished, real listings. It's kept here as a record of how pieces in this series come into being, not as a claim about their current status.

On the live store, this range also falls within the "2,001–2,500" collection grouping — part of how the full 10,000-piece run gets organized into browsable batches.
