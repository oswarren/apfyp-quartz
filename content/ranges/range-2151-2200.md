Forty-eight pieces from $21.51 to $22.00, with reserve gaps at 2181 and 2198. The range opens where the [[range-2101-2150|previous one]] left off but quickly settles into a long family of warm-white cups: a black-lava opener ([[2151]]), a plain oval-scarred cup ([[2152]]), and then the May-4 run that gathers iron into a new mark — the [[rust-streak|rust streak]], a rust-brown run down the wall that put `surface/rust-streak` in the registry across five cups ([[2157]], [[2158]], [[2163]], [[2165]], [[2166]]), with fainter wisps between them ([[2154]], [[2160]], [[2161]], [[2162]], [[2164]], [[2169]]). Breaking up the white are the batch's painted cups — bold black [[brushed-glaze|brushwork]] over blue on [[2159]], [[2167]], and [[2170]], a flooded black field on [[2172]], and the starry reactive turquoise of [[2173]] — and a matched pair pitted with small dark [[crater-cluster|burst-craters]] ([[2168]], [[2171]]), the second new term of the range.

Then the form loosens. The May-11 session brings a painterly [[2174|chawan run]] — cream tea bowls washed in indigo and crawled black, from gate-like brushed bars ([[2184]]) and gestural swirls ([[2185]]) to broad pitted lava patches ([[2180]], [[2182]]) — with one bare [[sand-grit|sand-grit]] outlier ([[2188]]) and a short cluster of coarse grogged tumblers ([[2189]]–[[2192]]) closing the day. The May-12 group then does something the range hadn't yet: it puts down the cup. Pieces [[2193]]–[[2200]] are the range's first [[2193|bowls]] — a wide brushed-patch pair ([[2193]], [[2195]]), a charcoal-coated deep bowl ([[2194]]), a quiet blue-grey wash ([[2196]]), a weathered mottle ([[2197]]), a gate-marked grogged bowl ([[2199]]), and a plain rough closer at the round $22.00 ([[2200]]). The photos span five dates — April 30, May 4, May 5, May 11, and May 12, 2026 — and every listing photo in the range was reviewed up close on 2026-07-13.
