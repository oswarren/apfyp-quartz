Some listing images carry real capture timestamps in their filenames, which lets those pieces be grouped by the day they were photographed. Two caveats, always: this is a **photography date** — not a claim about when a piece was made from clay or fired — and coverage is partial. 204 of the photographed pieces carry a date; pieces whose photos have no timestamp (most numbers below 1700) have no date here and appear only in [the Ledger](../pieces/).
