Every piece in this series carries the same recurring line in its listing description:

> "This is handmade piece {N} of 10,000 ceramic pieces in this project. As of this piece, my hands are {N}x more used to the touch of clay."

This page walks through eleven consecutive pieces in order, using that line as the thread connecting them.

1. [[2250]] — hands 2,250x more used to the touch of clay.
2. [[2251]] — 2,251x. Its number is hand-painted directly onto the base.
3. [[2252]] — 2,252x. A small abstract line mark appears inside.
4. [[2253]] — 2,253x. Held in hand in its photos — and the last of the hand-painted base numbers.
5. [[2254]] — 2,254x. The first incised base number — a shift in how the hands marked their work. A small interior mark echoes 2252's.
6. [[2255]] — 2,255x. A star-shaped cluster of speckling, seen from above.
7. [[2256]] — 2,256x. A more angular, faceted cup than the rounder ones before it.
8. [[2257]] — 2,257x.
9. [[2258]] — 2,258x. An interior line drawing, visible from one angle.
10. [[2259]] — 2,259x. The most photographed piece in the batch — six images of the same hands' work.
11. [[2260]] — 2,260x. The end of this walk, for now — hands 2,260 times more used to the touch of clay than when the project began.

Each stop links to its own page for the full visual description and a direct link to buy on Shopify.
